Who We Are

Schones Heim Builders is a premier construction and general contracting company based in Nairobi, Kenya. Founded in 2018, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner in the construction sector, delivering projects that stand the test of time.

Our team of skilled professionals brings together expertise in design, project management, and execution to ensure every project is completed to the highest standards of quality, safety, and sustainability.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to client satisfaction, transparent communication, and delivering value for money on every project we undertake across Nairobi and Kenya.
